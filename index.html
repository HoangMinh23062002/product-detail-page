Warranty policy


Warranty coverage
All XOR phone products are officially distributed in the Vietnam market via Gluxury.
Warranty period: 1 year from the date of purchase based on the Warranty Card.
The warranty period for repaired / replaced products
The warranty period for replacement spare parts is calculated as follows:
If the product has a warranty period of more than 6 months, the warranty period of the replacement spare part will be equal to the remaining warranty period of the product.
If the product has less than 6 months of the warranty period or was out of warranty, the warranty period of replacement parts will be 6 months.
Warranty period with renewed products
The warranty period of the renewed products is calculated as follows:
If the product has a warranty period of more than 6 months, the warranty period of the new equipment will be equal to the remaining warranty period of the product.
If the product has a warranty period of fewer than 6 months or is out of warranty, the warranty period of renewed equipment will be 6 months.
